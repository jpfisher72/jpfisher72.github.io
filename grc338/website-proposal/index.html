Introduction

Iron Haven

Iron Haven is a small gym that offers individual training, small group classes, and open gym access. They primarily specialize in free weights but also have all machines necessary to get a good workout.

Target audience

People who use this site are most likely looking for a gym to join, or are existing members looking for the class schedules

Non-Members: Looking for information about the gym such as what services are offered, what equipment they have, how much it costs, how to join, hours, etc. People looking at the website are most likely looking to improve their health and may have strength or physique goals

Members: Looking for class schedules and how to sign up for classes. Might also be looking for contact information for management

Home

Iron Haven is a small gym located in Town, CA. We specialize in helping our members reach their strength and physique goals through targeted individual and small group training, focusing on proper movement patterns to keep you in the gym and injury free. We also offer open gym access to all of our members with free reign over all of our equipment

Iron Haven offers 3 membership tiers:

Gold Member: Enjoy 3 weekly 1-on-1 personal training sessions with one of our certified personal trainers. Enrollment in our Gold Member program is limited! Gold members also enjoy unlimited small class enrollments and Open Gym access.

Silver Member: Enjoy unlimited access to our small group classes! Silver members also have unlimited access to Open Gym.

Bronze Member: This grants you unlimited access to Open Gym. It also grants you the ability to enroll in small group classes, but with a small fee.
