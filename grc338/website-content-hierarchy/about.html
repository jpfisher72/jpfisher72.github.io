About Us

There are many Italian Restaurants in San Luis Obispo, but if you're looking for a truly authentic Italian dining experience, then you should consider Jonathan's Meatballs Restaurant. Rated among the best San Luis Obispo restaurants, Jonathan's Meatballs uses the freshest, quality ingredients grown locally to create some of the most outstanding dishes you could find in San Luis Obispo eateries. For your convenience, Jonathan's Meatballs offers dine-in, takeout and delivery service. Jonathan's Meatballs is one of the few restaurants near Mission San Luis Obispo, and a block away from hotels in downtown San Luis Obispo to offer private dining options and banquet accommodations for corporate parties and family celebrations.

About Our Bakery

At Jonathan's Meatballs restaurant, we are well known for our freshly-baked garlic bread knots that are served with every dish and every salad you order at the restaurant. In addition to our famous garlic bread knots, we proudly bake all of our pizza doughs, sandwich breads, and all of our cookies and pastries right here at our bakery from scratch without any preservative 6 days a week. A variety of classic Italian style bite-sized cookies are available for sale at the store. Our cookies and biscottis are also available for purchase online.

About Chef Jonathan

Chef Jonathan started his culinary experience when he was only 2 years old helping his mother cook for family and friend in her small kitchen in Italy. He grew up in Bari, a city located in Puglia right on the heel of the boot. After working several years for a Pizza Place called Il Marinaio and for Restaurant II Faraone he decided to study at a culinary school and received a degree from the Italian Culinary Institute of Bari in Puglia, Italy. Jonathan spent seven years working and traveling in many cities and regions of Italy. Immediately after receiving his degree, he worked all over Europe including France, England and Germany. A few years later, he decided to open a restaurant in Dominican Republic.

In 1999 he moved to United State hired by Alfredo The Original of Rome in EPCOT Center Disney World Orlando. He was also chef at Tiramisu and Aura Restaurant in Miami Beach, Florida. Chef Jonathan then was proposed to move to California and after opening 3 Restaurants in Los Angeles, Pasadena, Covina and Lake Arrowhead, moved to San Luis Obispo in 2001 where he worked as Executive Chef of Benvenuti Restaurant for two years. Now with the passion taught by his mother and a vast experience from all over the world Chef Jonathan Fisher is here to serve San Luis Obispo with Jonathan's Meatballs, a restaurant and catering service specializing in Italian Gastronomy in an innovative mix of traditional and contemporary cuisine.
